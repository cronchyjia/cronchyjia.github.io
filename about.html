About Me

I grew up Victoria captivated by technology, design, psychology, and the interplay between them all.

My interest in human computer interactions guided me through a journey of information visualization, creativity, and computer science. As I navigate through my career I am inspired by pioneering accessible technologies that benefit humanity, and the importance of diverse perspectives.

I've always loved helping people come up with solutions to problems; putting myself in other's shoes has always been very natural to me. Throughout my life I've encountered so many pieces of technology and programs that aren't intuitive and frustrating to use, because of that I want to be able to alleviate all these issues. I like being able to bridge the gap of "don't listen to what users say, listen to what they do".
